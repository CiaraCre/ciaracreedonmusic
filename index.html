About Ciara

A voice to remember

Hi, I'm Ciara — an experienced singer based in Cork, Ireland. I have a BA in Music from UCC and I am a qualified music teacher. I play guitar and piano, and I have performed in many churches, hotels and venues across Munster.

I have a passion for bringing music to life on special days. I'll work with you to craft the perfect soundtrack for your ceremony — whether that's a joyful wedding or a tender farewell.

I perform across all genres and take pride in learning songs that are personally meaningful to each couple or family. Every performance is tailored, rehearsed, and delivered with warmth and professionalism.

Funeral Music

A tender farewell

Music can bring great comfort at a time of loss. I offer sensitive, heartfelt performances for funeral and memorial ceremonies across Munster and beyond — in churches, crematoriums, and gravesides.

I understand that every person's life is unique, and I'll work with you and your family to choose music that truly honours their memory. I'm happy to learn a specific song that was meaningful to your loved one.

Please don't hesitate to get in touch — I'll do everything I can to help at short notice.
